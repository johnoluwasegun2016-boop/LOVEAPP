Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2026

Welcome to Perfect Love Text. Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Perfect Love Text is designed as a **privacy-first application**. We operate under the philosophy that your private feelings, memories, and messages should remain entirely yours.

  • LocalStorage Storage: Any information you enter within the app, including **Secret Notes, Couples Milestones, Anniversary Dates, Daily Challenges status, and Captured Memories**, is stored locally on your own physical device via browser LocalStorage.
  • No Server Database: We do not operate databases or external servers that store your content. Once you close the app, your data remains secure on your local device storage.
  • No Personal Information: We do not ask for registration, signup, phone numbers, emails, or personal accounts.

2. Third-Party Services & Analytics

Our app does not implement third-party analytics packages, trackers, or SDKs that scrape device identifiers or monitor browser patterns. Our text synthesis, countdowns, and daily challenges are computed locally on your client engine.

3. Text-to-Speech Engine

The **Voice Dedication** features use the Web Speech API provided natively by your browser/operating system. The synthesis process occurs locally on your device; no audio files or spoken phrases are transmitted to external servers.

4. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect, process, or transmit personal data, our app does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. Anyone of appropriate age to express their feelings can safely use this application.

5. Security of Your Data

Because your data is stored locally on your device, the security of your entries (including Secret Notes locked by a 4-digit PIN) depends entirely on the physical and passcode security of your own device. We recommend securing your browser and device with passcodes or biometric lock systems.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, you can reach out to our team at support@perfectlovetext.example.com.
